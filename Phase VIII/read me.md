## Problem Statement
The Hospital Equipment Management System addresses the critical need for automated tracking and maintenance scheduling of medical equipment in healthcare facilities. Manual systems lead to equipment downtime, missed maintenance, and inefficient resource allocation, ultimately affecting patient care quality.

## Key Objectives
1. Automate equipment lifecycle management
2. Schedule preventive maintenance efficiently
3. Monitor equipment status in real-time
4. Generate comprehensive maintenance reports
5. Implement business intelligence for decision support
6. Ensure data security and audit compliance
